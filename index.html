Gear-Up

Gear Up is a web app that allows automotive shops tp automate reservation and the selling of mechanical parts and autos in the market. it has a feature of creating an account for users to be able to have reservation and have a dashboard for checking out items. Home Page of users have a carousel of items such as cars and parts for sale. Gear Up also comes with an admin page where the admin can see reservations and orders from the users.
